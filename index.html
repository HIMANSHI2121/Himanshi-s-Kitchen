Himanshi's Recipe

Veg Chowmein

Veg chowmein's

Recipe

Cooking time

Atleast 30 minutes.
Servings

5-6 persons
Ingredients

  • 200gms Noodles
  • 1 Medium sized chopped Onion
  • 1 sliced Carrot
  • 1 cup shredded Cabbage
  • 2 sliced Capsicum
  • 1 tablespoon Soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Chopped Garlic
  • 2 tablespoon Cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon Black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Salt or to taste
How to make

  • Boil water and add 1 tbsn.oil and salt then add noodles.
  • Rinse the boiled noodles with cold water.
  • Heat a pan and add 1 tbsn. oil to it.
  • Add garlic to it, then add onion, carrot, cabbage and capsicum to the pan.
  • Saute for 2-3 min.
  • Now, Add noodles, black pepper, salt, soy sauce and red chilli sauce.
  • Mix and saute them for 3-5 min.
  • Add vinegar to it and serve.
